Clara Wieck ( Leipzig , 13 of September of 1819 - Frankfurt Meno , 20 of maypole of 1896 )

Clara Wieck

The 19th century's most important woman pianist, composer

Known as Clara Schumann(Clara Wieck) was a pianist, composer and piano teacher German. She was one of the great European concert performers of the 19th century and her career was key in spreading the compositions of her husband, Robert Schumann. Considered one of the most distinguished pianists of the romantic era, influenced a 61-year concert career, and changed the format and repertoire of the piano recital from exhibitions from virtuosity to serious works programs. He also composed solo piano pieces, a Piano Concerto ( op . 7), chamber music, choral pieces, and songs.
